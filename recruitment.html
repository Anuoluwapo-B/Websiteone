ABOUT IRS


Our Values

At Inspire we believe in acting in a transparent manner keeping all parties involved in the recruitment solution informed in an open and honest manner. With ethics at our core, we are passionate, committed and ambitious in our approach to delivering the solutions our clients and candidates demand from us.

Our Team

We have a core dedicated and highly motivated team of recruitment consultants to provide our clients and candidates with high quality value for money service with emphasis on developing long lasting personal relationships with our clients and candidates. We are members of the Recruitment and Employment Confederation (REC), and as such you can be confident that our recruitment service is delivered to the highest standards.

Our Candidates

Inspire has built a vast candidate database over the years. We aware that the way people search for jobs is constantly changing and therefore we use all types of media to attract our candidates from, word of mouth, direct marketing, online networking sites to referral schemes.

No success No fee

Clients do not have to pay service charge for permanent staff enquiries unless you engage an applicant introduced by us. Additionally not only does our service take away the need to sift through scores of CV's but we offer a generous rebate scheme that gives you protection should the employee leave within the guarantee period (probationary period).
