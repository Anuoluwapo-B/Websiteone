ABOUT NLPC

Home >> About Us

The North London Partnership Consortium (NLPC) is a dynamic and innovative urban regeneration and renewal organisation. We are dedicated to enhancing business competitiveness and improving the social and economic welfare of individuals.

Founded in 2000, the organisation has been at the forefront of providing creative management solutions to employers across sectors, and helping unemployed people back into work.Our work involves developing, implementing and managing practical solutions for clients.We have a highly motivated and skilled team and believe in applying a professional approach, that is innovative, value for money and responsive to clients’ needs.

Over the years we have developed key branded service provisions in the following:

1. Recruitment and Employment Services delivered through our sector brand Inspire Recruitment Solutions.

2. Welfare to Work Projects and Training Services delivered through NLPC.

3. Research and Consultancy delivered through our successful innovative brand Regenesys Associates Consultancy.
